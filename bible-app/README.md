# 📖 Bilingual Bible App (Amharic & English)

A beautiful, offline-capable Bible application built with Flutter. This app features both the Amharic Bible and the King James Version (KJV) in English, completely free and accessible without an internet connection.

## ✨ Features

- **🌍 Bilingual Support:** Read the Bible in both Amharic and English (KJV).
- **✈️ Offline First:** All Bible chapters and verses are bundled locally. You can read the word of God anywhere, anytime, without needing an internet connection.
- **🌙 Day & Night Mode:** A built-in dark mode toggle to provide a comfortable reading experience in low-light environments.
- **⚡ Fast & Responsive:** Built with Flutter for smooth scrolling and instant chapter navigation.
- **🔍 Simple Navigation:** Easy-to-use interface to quickly jump between books, chapters, and verses.
